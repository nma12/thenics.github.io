2002

[ THE ORIGIN ]

In 2002, I conceived a dream to “build an end-time army.”

2013

[ THE JOURNEY ]

In 2013, this dream was birthed as The Nazirite Homeschool, with my own children as the first students.

As a stay-at-home mother at the time, with a passion to raise and nurture children in the fear and ways of the Lord, our home education journey—using the ACE Curriculum—took centre stage in our lives.

EXP.

[ THE CALLING ]

A few years later, the Lord opened doors for me to serve as a principal and director in two different Christian international schools. This, together with 18 years of homeschooling my children, gave me valuable experience in education and leadership that I greatly needed for the calling ahead of me.

2023

[ THE OPENING ]

The opening of Nazirite International Christian School to friends and neighbours in 2023 was the result. The school continues to grow.

NEXT

[ THE FUTURE ]

As we look to the future, we remain dedicated and committed to raising children with an undivided consecration to God and an excellent education that springs from this commitment.
