2013

[ BLOCK 001 : THE ORIGIN ]

Twelve years ago, in 2013, a dream that was conceived 26 years ago, 'to build Him an end-time generation' was birthed. The Nazirites home school, with my own children as students, was born in my living room.

PREP

[ BLOCK 002 : THE JOURNEY ]

As a stay-at-home mother then, with a passion to raise and nurture children in the fear and ways of the Lord, our home education journey, using the ACE Curriculum, began.

EXP.

[ BLOCK 003 : THE CALLING ]

The Lord opened a door for me to serve as a principal and later director and principal in two different Christian international schools. This gave me a great experience in education and leadership that I greatly needed for the calling ahead of me.

"My experience as a stay-at-home mother for 18 years, educating my own children from home for 10 years, and the opportunity to serve as principal in two other Christian schools for about five years prepared me for the calling to raise an end-time generation."

2023

[ BLOCK 004 : THE OPENING ]

This led to the opening up of The Nazarite International Christian School to friends and neighbors in 2023 with the first 10 students. The school continues to grow.

NEXT

[ BLOCK 005 : THE FUTURE ]

As we look into the future, we remain dedicated and committed to raising children that have an undivided consecration to God and an excellent education springing from this commitment.
