COMMUNITY & CONNECTION

PARENT
ENGAGEMENTS

01

Parents’ Prayer Altar

[ Click to Expand ]

We believe that, as parents, God is in charge of our lives and leads our every step toward fulfilling our divine destiny. Therefore, we prioritise praying together as a family on a weekly basis.

02

Teacher–Parent Conferencing

[ Click to Expand ]

We create a termly opportunity for parents to meet classroom teachers and have ample time to talk with and discuss their students’ moral and academic progress with the appropriate teachers, for the purpose of academic and character growth.

03

Parenting Webinars

[ Click to Expand ]

We invite different experienced trainers and pastors to share, inspire, and equip parents with sound parenting principles for raising our children in the ways of the Lord and in essential daily life skills.

04

Parents’ Listening Day

[ Click to Expand ]

Children grow wiser, feel more understood, and learn to communicate better when parents prioritise listening to them. Therefore, we create an opportunity once every term for parents to come and listen to the concerns in their children’s lives. This fosters good communication, reduces stress in children, and strengthens family bonding.

05

Mentors / Minions Programme

[ Click to Expand ]

Each learner at TNICS is given the opportunity to have a mentor who walks a journey with them throughout their time at the school. This fosters a teachable spirit and enhances respect for elders and humility among growing children.
