OUR

PHILOSOPHY

At The Nazirite International Christian School, we believe that God is the ultimate source of all truth and knowledge, and that learning is an act that honors and glorifies Him. Guided by this belief, we engage our students in a challenging and enriching academic program, using a variety of instructional approaches designed to cultivate academic growth.

[ THE ACADEMIC SEQUENCE ]

A seamless progression from foundational learning to university-ready excellence.

The Nazirite International Christian School academic sequence uses diverse instructional approaches and rigorous curricula across grade levels to support effective and meaningful learning.

01

Early Years & Elementary

PRE-K to GRADE 6 • FOUNDATIONS & MASTERY

Our early years and elementary school programs focus on building strong foundational skills in comprehension and goal setting. The ACE curriculum supports this through structured independence, self-paced mastery, and intentional biblical integration.

02

Middle School

GRADES 7-8 • APPLICATION & CRITICAL THINKING

At the middle school level, students engage with challenging, practical American Christian curricula that emphasize application, collaboration, and critical thinking. They progress beyond memorization to analysis, evaluation, and creative problem-solving across all subjects.

03

High School

GRADES 9-12 • HIGH SCHOOL DIPLOMA & A-LEVELS

Our high school offers a rigorous and globally focused education, combining a broad American curriculum in Grades 9 and 10 with International A Levels in Grades 11 and 12. This high school diploma program develops critical thinking, analytical reasoning, problem-solving skills, and effective communication skills. Graduates leave well-prepared for universities around the world, equipped with both a U.S. high school diploma and internationally recognized A-level qualifications.
