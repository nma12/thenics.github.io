AFTER-SCHOOL
CLUBS

/// EXTRACURRICULAR

[ OVERVIEW ] The Nazirite International Christian School After-School Clubs offer students a vibrant space to explore their interests, develop new skills, and build confidence beyond the classroom. Our diverse range of clubs ensures that every student can find something they are passionate about while learning the value of teamwork, creativity, and commitment.

01

Performing Arts

DANCE • MUSIC • THEATRE

Students can express themselves through Dance, Music, and Theatre, where performance, confidence, and artistic talent are nurtured.

02

Creative Writing

BOOK CLUB

For those who love words and stories, Creative Writing/Book Club provides a welcoming environment to read, write, and share ideas.

03

Cookery

COOKING

Hands-on learners can enjoy experimenting with recipes, learning new cooking methods, and creating delicious dishes together.

04

Robotics

STEM • INNOVATION

Robotics challenges students to think critically, innovate, and solve real-world problems using technology.

05

Yearbook/Journalism

MEDIA • DESIGN

The Yearbook/Journalism Club allows students to document school life, develop writing and design skills, and preserve memories that last a lifetime.

06

Aviation Club

FLIGHT • AERONAUTICS

Aviation Club introduces learners to the exciting world of flight, engineering, and aeronautics, sparking curiosity about STEM careers.

07

Gaming Club

STRATEGY • COLLABORATION

Gaming Club promotes strategic thinking, collaboration, and responsible digital engagement in a fun and social setting.

08

African Perspectives

HISTORY • CULTURE

The African Perspectives Club explores history from an African point of view, encouraging students to better understand Africa’s role in world history.

"Each year, TNICS After-school Clubs proudly host a variety of events that showcase students’ talents and hard work. These include performances, exhibitions, competitions, showcases, and themed activity days."
