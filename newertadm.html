/// PROTOCOLS & GUIDELINES ///

Criteria for Admission

01

We consider students whose academic goals align with the school expectation of great intellectual prowess.

02

We consider students whose academic and co-curricular goals would benefit with the schools offerings.

03

We consider students who show evidence of academic potential and excellence able to handle our rigorous curriculum and critical thinking.

04

We believe in parental partnership. At least one parent should typically provide a clear testimony of faith and agree to partner with the school in the child's spiritual and academic growth.

05

We consider the space available in the grade being applied for.

06

We believe in parental partnership. Therefore, at least one parent should provide a clear testimony of faith and agree to partner with the school in the child's spiritual and academic growth.

Sequence of Entry

01

Application Form

Parents are required to complete and submit the school application form. All applications are carefully reviewed and assessed by the admissions team.

02

Official School Visit

Families invited to proceed will attend an official school visit, which will be scheduled via email or phone call to tour our campus and facilities.

03

Student Interview

The prospective student will attend an interview with a member of the admissions team. This interaction allows us to assess character and potential.

04

Parent Interview

Parents or guardians will also participate in an interview as part of the admission process to ensure a strong partnership between home and school values.

[ Confirmation ]

Admission will be confirmed upon completion of the assessments and payment of the admission fee.

[ POST-INTERVIEW ]

Assessment

After completing the specified steps, shortlisted students will sit for assessments in Mathematics, English, and Comprehension.
